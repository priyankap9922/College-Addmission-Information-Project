* Department Of Civil Engineering *

The Department of Civil Engineering was established in the year 1988. The graduate Program B.E. Automobile was introduced in 1985. At present, the Department offers Graduate Program in Civil Engineering, Post Graduate Program Civil Engineering. Our Department holds a good reputation for producing eminent engineers as professionals, entrepreneurs and researchers. Many of our alumni occupy key positions in industries as well as in institutions across India and abroad.
