* Department Of Electrical Engineering *

The Department of Electrical Engineering was established in the year 1992. The graduate Program B.E. Electrical was introduced in 1996. At present, the Department offers Graduate Program in Electrical Engineering, Post Graduate Program Electrical Engineering. Our Department holds a good reputation for producing eminent engineers as professionals, entrepreneurs and researchers. Many of our alumni occupy key positions in industries as well as in institutions across India and abroad.
