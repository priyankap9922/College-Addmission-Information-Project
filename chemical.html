* Department Of Chemical Engineering *

The Department of Chemical Engineering was established in the year 2005. The graduate Program B.E. Chemical was introduced in 2007. At present, the Department offers Graduate Program in Chemical Engineering, Post Graduate Program Chemical Engineering. Our Department holds a good reputation for producing eminent engineers as professionals, entrepreneurs and researchers. Many of our alumni occupy key positions in industries as well as in institutions across India and abroad.
