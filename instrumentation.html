* Department Of instrumentation Engineering *

The Department of instrumentation Engineering was established in the year 2008. The graduate Program B.E. instrumentation was introduced in 2009. At present, the Department offers Graduate Program in instrumentation Engineering, Post Graduate Program instrumentation Engineering. Our Department holds a good reputation for producing eminent engineers as professionals, entrepreneurs and researchers. Many of our alumni occupy key positions in industries as well as in institutions across India and abroad.
