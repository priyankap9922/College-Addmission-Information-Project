* Department Of Automobile Engineering *

The Department of Automobile Engineering was established in the year 2002. The graduate Program B.E. Automobile was introduced in 2000. At present, the Department offers Graduate Program in Automobile Engineering, Post Graduate Program Automobile Engineering. Our Department holds a good reputation for producing eminent engineers as professionals, entrepreneurs and researchers. Many of our alumni occupy key positions in industries as well as in institutions across India and abroad.

VISION

  • To be a leading department for providing outstanding Automobile engineering education and facilitating research.

  • To strengthen the facilities and conducive atmosphere in promoting quality education in multidisciplinary area.
