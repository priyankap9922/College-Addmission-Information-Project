* Department Of Computer Engineering *

The Department of Computer Engineering was established in the year 1992. The graduate Program B.E. Computer was introduced in 1996. At present, the Department offers Graduate Program in Computer Engineering, Post Graduate Program Computer Engineering. Our Department holds a good reputation for producing eminent engineers as professionals, entrepreneurs and researchers. Many of our alumni occupy key positions in industries as well as in institutions across India and abroad.
